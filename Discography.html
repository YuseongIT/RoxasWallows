Albums and Singles (in chronological order) Description

Pleaser (2017)

“Pleaser” is not a new song, nor is it underground. Yet, there is something so perfect about this song that has resonated with me ever since I first heard it. This song was one that marked Wallows’ debut and made the band who they are today. Who would have thought back in 2017 that their debut single would be such a revolutionary moment for them in the years to follow? The track is relatable in its entirety. About a guy who always finds himself biting his tongue and avoiding saying what’s on his mind to make sure other people are happy. Might sound familiar to those of us that are people-pleasers.


"Pleaser" - Wallows

Spring EP (2018)

"Spring" is the debut extended play (EP) by American band Wallows. It was released on April 6, 2018 through Atlantic Records. On February 23, 2018, Wallows released the album's first single "Pictures of Girls"; a subsequent music video was released the same day. The album's second single "These Days" was released on March 23, 2018. On June 25, 2018, a music video for "These Days" was released and is currently the second most popular video on the Wallows YouTube channel.

Its songs include:

  • Ground
  • It's Only Right
  • Let The Sun In
  • These Days
  • Pictures Of Girls

"Pictures of Girls" - Wallows

Nothing Happens (2019)

"Nothing Happens" is the debut studio album by the American band Wallows, released on March 22, 2019, through Atlantic Records. The album received generally positive reviews from critics. Gisselle Pernett of Rock Cellar Magazine praised its mix of dance-worthy and nostalgic songs. Olivia Kesling from The Alternative found it entertaining with infectious energy. Matt Raven of Under the Radar likened the musical arrangements to The Shins and Death Cab for Cutie.

Its songs include:

  • Are You Bored Yet?
  • Treacherous Doctor
  • Remember When
  • Ice Cold Pool
  • Scrawny

"Scrawny" - Wallows

Tell Me That It's Over (2022)

In September 2021, Wallows released the lead single, "I Don't Want to Talk," accompanied by a music video and North American tour dates. On December 5, 2021, they unveiled the album title and previewed four audio track snippets on a new website. The second single, "Especially You," and its music video were released on February 3, 2022, along with international tour dates, a tracklist, cover, and the release date for the album, "Tell Me That It's Over." The third single, "At the End of the Day," dropped on March 4, 2022, featuring a music video shot in Las Vegas. The album's fifth track, "Marvelous," also had its music video released alongside the album on March 5, 2022. Jason Lester directed all the music videos for "Tell Me That It's Over."

Its songs include:

  • Hard To Believe
  • Marvelous
  • Permanent Price
  • That's What I Get
  • I Don't Want To Talk

"I Don't Want To Talk" - Wallows
