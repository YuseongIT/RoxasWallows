Who are the Wallows?

"Wallows" is an American alternative rock band based in Los Angeles composed of Dylan Minnette, Braeden Lemasters, and Cole Preston. The band began releasing songs independently in April 2017, starting with "Pleaser", which reached number two on the Spotify Global Viral 50 chart.

Wallows' musical style has mainly been described as alternative rock, post-punk, power pop, indie rock, bedroom pop, indie pop and surf punk. Wallows have cited Arctic Monkeys, The Strokes, The Libertines, Arcade Fire, Kanye West, and The Smiths as some of the band's artistic influences.

The Alternative Rock Genre

In addition to their musical prowess, Wallows' commitment to authenticity and storytelling sets them apart. Their lyrics, often introspective and relatable, delve into the human experience, creating a profound connection with listeners. The band's ability to infuse raw emotion into their compositions adds a layer of depth to the Alternative and Indie Rock genres.
